About

We're a registered accelerator partnering with founders at the earliest stages — providing capital, hands-on mentorship, and access to a unique industrial network.

Who We Are

Ryukyung Investment is a Korean accelerator (창업기획자) registered with the Ministry of SMEs and Startups, and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ryukyung Industrial — a Korean enterprise with over six decades of operating heritage.

We invest in Seed and Pre-Series A startups with the conviction to back them through Series A and beyond. Our capital comes from both our balance sheet and personal investment funds we organize.

What sets us apart is the depth of our network. Through our affiliates — including a leading asset manager, a venture firm, and a private equity partner — founders gain access to capital, expertise, and relationships that meaningfully accelerate their journey.
